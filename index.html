Montenegro Ploce Beach

2014-2019 Summer

Montenegro isn't very well known and it's pretty small on the map. But I've been to Montenegro so many times and trust me, it's beautiful. Yes, Montenegro is where my parents come from and serbian is my home language. Just because I'm basically from Montenegro, doesn't mean I'll make up stuff to make it seem so great, I mean it.

There are gorgeous never ending mountains...

Awesome fun cities...

Stunning blue beaches...

Lands full of nature, agriculture & fresh air...

But as you know, today I'll be talking about Ploce beach and the countless memories it brings back.
I remember, me and my family loved that place and we still do. It's our #1 beach destination where we like to spend 7-10 days. We find a hotel or rent a house close to Ploce. Then it usually takes 5 minutes by car to get there. We go 1-2 times/day because it never gets boring!
 You can go to their restaurant that consists of delicious food and an awesome beach view. You can enjoy some fun activities like parasailing, jetskiing, towable (sofa), etc. I know some people prefer to go to the pool instead of the salty beach, so you can even swim in Ploce's kid or adult pool. Ploce isn't very known around Montenegro so you won't find many photos, but I recommend to keep searching. It's a great rocky looking beach.
Me and my family love to invite some friends over who were either from Montenegro, or came to visit it like us! My family's favorite activity was jumping off a cliff right into the water. It might sound crazy, but don't worry, it wasn't that high. However people did flips, dives, spins, cannonballs or just some regular jumps. To be honest, I wasn't as brave for the flips. I did some dives but in the lower area, and you can say I tried to do a backflip. I'll get better next year!

Great Wolf Lodge

2013 & 2015 & 2017 Winter

Great Wolf Lodge is an amazing water park,especially for kids. This is a really great place to look back to one day as a family memory. You may go there for a fun, warm and relaxing winter. Or an outdoor fun hot summer. Maybe even your birthday.
It consists of a gift store, daily storytelling event, wizard quest, cozy main room, single water rides, duo water rides, family water rides, water slides, kid parks, a surfing area, wave pool, restaurants, an arcade, a bowling room, a spa and your cozy rooms. Such a memorable place!

Here are some pictures to get to know Great Wolf Lodge a little better...

Personally, I think Great Wolf Lodge is such a fun and cozy place. I prefer to go during the winter because it's all fun and warm indoor while outside it's all snowy. The second I walked in, I got this cozy at home feeling. The worker put a coloured plastic bracelet on our hands as he/she measured our height. It wasn't any kind of bracelet, there was a built in fob that could only open our apartment door! The room we chose had a bunkbed. So me and my brother "fought" for it. We might go next year because we miss Great Wolf Lodge, even though we're all "grown up". So there will be a lot of thinking on who had the top bunk last (2017).
Some of the rides were chill, like the flowing water, where you just layed in your water tube (alone or with someone) as the water pushed you along the tour. Then there are the family rides, like the Wooly Mamooth that casually swung side to side pretty fast. I also remember a ride my family likes to call the Toilet ride, it's where you are in a tube in the dark, then all of a sudden the area opens up as you slide around the funnel and "flush" down the hole. We can't forget the arcade, there were so many great games and prizes!
I 100% recommend Great Wolf Lodge for you and your family and/or friends!!
